SECTION TO ADD: FIREBASE AUTHENTICATION & CLOUD STORAGE
================================================================================

We use Firebase by Google to provide account management and cloud save features.

DATA WE COLLECT THROUGH FIREBASE:

1. ACCOUNT INFORMATION:
   • Email Address - For login and account recovery
   • Password - Stored securely using Firebase Authentication (hashed with bcrypt,
     never stored in plain text)
   • Username - Optional display name or extracted from email
   • User ID (UID) - Unique identifier generated by Firebase

2. GOOGLE SIGN-IN (if you use Google login):
   • Google profile name
   • Google profile picture
   • Google email address

3. GAME PROGRESS DATA (stored in Firebase Firestore):
   • Virtual currency balances (BTC, ETH, DOGE, USD)
   • Upgrades, skills, and achievements
   • Statistics (total earned, total spent, total clicks, play time)
   • Staking amounts and rewards
   • Account creation date and last login time

4. TECHNICAL DATA (Firebase Analytics):
   • IP address (for security/fraud prevention)
   • Device type and browser
   • Approximate location (country/region, not precise GPS)
   • Usage statistics

HOW WE PROTECT YOUR DATA:

✓ Passwords are HASHED using bcrypt - never stored in plain text
✓ All data transmission uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
✓ Stored on Google's secure Firebase infrastructure
✓ Server-side validation prevents data tampering
✓ Regular automated backups

WE DO NOT SELL YOUR DATA:
• Your personal information is NEVER sold to third parties
• We DO NOT share your data for marketing purposes
• Firebase is used solely to provide game services
• Google may use anonymized/aggregated data for service improvements only

YOUR RIGHTS:
• Access your data at any time
• Request data export by emailing idlebtcminer@gmail.com
• Delete your account and all data by contacting us
• Opt out of analytics cookies via browser settings

DATA RETENTION:
• Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
• Inactive accounts: May be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
• Deleted accounts: All data permanently removed within 30 days

Purpose: Authentication, cloud database, analytics
Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
Data Location: Google servers worldwide
